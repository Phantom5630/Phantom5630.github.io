GAME INTRODUCTION

INFAMY

2D Sidescroll | Strategy | Minigames

Play as an unemployed guy, who stream himself doing petty crimes.

Objective of the game is to meet the given quota of viewers within time limit.

TRY OUT NOW!

    Additional Info

  • Outcome of my GPS1 (Group Project Studio 1) course.
  • My first time officially working with other programmer to make a game.

TUTORIAL

Tutorial Part 1

Tutorial Part 2

  • Camera will focus on target object when it is mentioned.
  • Some parts will require player to complete certain action to proceed and the dialogue box will switch to a window after a few seconds.

EQUIPMENT SYSTEM

Equipment Switching

  • Changing clothes only swap the sprite for specific parts.
  • The changes in stats after switching equipment are displayed as the fading part of the bars.
  • Whatever bought or equipped will be carried on throughout the game.

UPGRADES SYSTEM

Upgrades

  • All upgrades will be carried on throughout the game.

HIDING CONTROL AND MECHANIC

Hiding Mechanic

  • Hide to avoid patrolling cops.
  • Cops can't get you when you're hidden, unless you try to hide while they're watching.

STREAM CHAT

In-game Stream Chat

  • Viewers will 'chat' while you're doing your job.
  • The viewers' usernames and chat contents are randomized from respective array.

TRANSITIONS APPLICATION

Main Transition

In-game Transition

  • Main transition will be played whenever player proceed to next scene.
  • For travelling to streets or stores, in-game transition will be played instead.
